Nowhere Here

Alternative dance space exploration - state based research.

In Nowhere Here, Juolin explores a sense of disconnection between the reality and her existence.

She interacts with the objects in the Dance Centre 5th floor lounge

with a sense of alienation and confusion.

She questions life and death, familiarity and loss as she moves through the physical space.
